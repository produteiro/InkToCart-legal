Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 29, 2026

InkToCart (“we”, “our”, or “the app”) is a simple utility application that helps users convert handwritten shopping lists into interactive digital checklists using AI.

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting any information collected through the app.

1. Information We Collect

  • Camera and Photos: When you take a photo or import an image from your gallery, it is sent temporarily to OpenAI only for text recognition and categorization. The original image is not stored by us after processing.
  • Local Data: All your checklists are stored locally on your device.

We do not collect name, email, location, or any personally identifiable information.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information solely to convert your handwritten list into a digital checklist and to improve the accuracy of text recognition and categorization.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your data for marketing purposes. The only third-party service used is OpenAI for image processing.

4. Data Retention

Images are deleted immediately after processing. Your checklists remain on your device until you delete them.

5. Your Rights

You can delete any checklist at any time, revoke camera permission in your device settings, or uninstall the app to remove all local data.
